Welcome to VegeVeganPlanet. This is your one stop website to explore the most delicious Vegan and Vegetarian food the world has to offer. Simple, wholesome food that can, literally, save the Earth. Step by step recipes presented with an understanding of how what we eat can positively influence the world around us. This is the website for the aspiring, as well as the committed Vegetarian or Vegan; for the ecologically concerned, as well as for the eco warrior; for those who simply love their pets, to those who wouldn’t hurt a fly! Here we appreciate every little change you make; from no meat Monday to Veganuary, from flexitarian to vegetarian and vegan. Our aim is to inspire you to your own personal change and we will do this by tempting you with healthy vegetarian and vegan food and with positive encouragement. Take a look around the site. There is loads on offer. We have recipes, environmental information, plus details of our restaurant and partner recommendations. What’s more, we have our very own YouTube channel where we will take you through some mouth-watering VegeVegan food preparation and hopefully keep you entertained and informed along the way. Heal yourself to heal the planet. Save an animal to save the world. Read more ...
