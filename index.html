Environment

Vegan food is not only the healthy choice it is also the option for those who care about the future of the planet. The environmental impact of meat production is manifold. As world population continues to increase so to has the consumption of meat and animal products. This has resulted in a quarter of the Earth’s available land being used to graze animals, whilst a third of the worlds arable land is used in feed crop production. The associated environmental problems include the overuse of fresh water, green house gas emissions, energy use, pollution of land and watercourses, and dramatic falls in the Earth’s biodiversity due to habitat destruction. At Vegan Planet we recognise that any changes we make towards a plant based diet not only helps ourselves but also safeguards this beautiful Earth for future generations. Read more ...

Health

A plant-based diet has myriad positive health effects on the body and mind. Vegan foods are rich in the nutrients your body needs to grow and flourish. The powerful antioxidant properties of many vegetables also significantly reduce the risks of cancer and other common diseases. From type 2 diabetes to a maintaining a healthy heart and reducing the inflammation of arthritis vegan foods are superfoods. Not only that, eating vegan will make you look good and feel good with healthy skin and a proven effect on your emotional feelgood factor. All in all, if you want to come into your full potential as a person, a vegan lifestyle is for you. Read more ...
